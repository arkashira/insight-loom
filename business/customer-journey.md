# customer-journey.md

## Customer Journey Map for Insight-Loom

### 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Founders and teams experience frustration due to disorganized information and lack of clear insights.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty in identifying the right tools to manage information; overwhelmed by the number of options available.
- **User Emotions**: Confusion, frustration, and a sense of urgency to find a solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational content (blogs, webinars) on the importance of organized information and insights; showcase testimonials from other founders who have benefited.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

### 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Users begin researching potential solutions and comparing features.
- **Friction Points**: Information overload from competing products; uncertainty about which features are most relevant to their needs.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism, cautious optimism, and analysis paralysis.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized demos or consultations; create comparison charts that highlight Insight-Loom’s unique benefits.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and product comparisons downloaded.

### 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a free trial or demo of Insight-Loom.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; learning curve associated with the new tool.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful, but anxious about the effectiveness of the tool.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide onboarding support, tutorials, and a user-friendly interface; offer quick wins within the first few days of use.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement during the trial period.

### 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users decide to purchase a subscription after the trial period.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about ongoing costs and ROI; fear of commitment to a new tool.
- **User Emotions**: Relief mixed with apprehension about the investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer flexible pricing plans; provide case studies demonstrating ROI; ensure seamless transition from trial to paid subscription.
- **Metric**: Subscription conversion rate and customer satisfaction score post-purchase.

### 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users see value in Insight-Loom and consider upgrading their plan or adding features.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about which additional features would provide the most value; potential budget constraints.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement about possibilities, but cautious about spending more.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide tailored recommendations for feature upgrades based on usage data; offer loyalty discounts or bundled packages.
- **Metric**: Upgrade rate and average revenue per user (ARPU).
