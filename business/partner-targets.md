# Partner Targets
## Overview
As a product that helps founders and teams to organize and analyze scattered information, Insight Loom will benefit from integrating with various SaaS and APIs to expand its capabilities and reach a broader audience.

## Partner Integration Roadmap
The following SaaS and APIs will be integrated with Insight Loom, prioritizing those with affiliate/revenue-share opportunities:

### 1. Notion (Integration Effort: M, Value-Add: Data Organization)
* Free-Tier Limit: 100 pages, 100 blocks
* Integration Rationale: Notion is a popular note-taking and collaboration platform. Integrating with Notion will allow users to import their existing data and connect their workflows.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes, Notion has an affiliate program

### 2. Airtable (Integration Effort: M, Value-Add: Data Analysis)
* Free-Tier Limit: 1,200 records, 24-hour sync limit
* Integration Rationale: Airtable is a cloud-based database that allows users to create custom tables and forms. Integrating with Airtable will enable users to connect their data sources and perform advanced analysis.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes, Airtable has an affiliate program

### 3. Google Drive (Integration Effort: L, Value-Add: File Storage)
* Free-Tier Limit: 15 GB
* Integration Rationale: Google Drive is a popular cloud storage service. Integrating with Google Drive will allow users to store and access their files directly within Insight Loom.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: No

### 4. Trello (Integration Effort: S, Value-Add: Project Management)
* Free-Tier Limit: Unlimited boards, lists, and cards
* Integration Rationale: Trello is a visual project management tool. Integrating with Trello will enable users to connect their workflows and track progress.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes, Trello has an affiliate program

### 5. Zapier (Integration Effort: M, Value-Add: Automation)
* Free-Tier Limit: 100 tasks/month, 5 zaps
* Integration Rationale: Zapier is an automation tool that connects various web applications. Integrating with Zapier will enable users to automate repetitive tasks and workflows.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes, Zapier has an affiliate program

### 6. Calendly (Integration Effort: S, Value-Add: Scheduling)
* Free-Tier Limit: 1 user, 50 meetings/month
* Integration Rationale: Calendly is a scheduling tool that allows users to share their availability. Integrating with Calendly will enable users to schedule meetings and appointments directly within Insight Loom.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes, Calendly has an affiliate program

### 7. Typeform (Integration Effort: M, Value-Add: Form Building)
* Free-Tier Limit: 1,000 responses/month
* Integration Rationale: Typeform is a form-building tool that allows users to create custom forms. Integrating with Typeform will enable users to collect data and feedback directly within Insight Loom.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes, Typeform has an affiliate program

### 8. Slack (Integration Effort: L, Value-Add: Communication)
* Free-Tier Limit: 10,000 messages, 10,000 files
* Integration Rationale: Slack is a popular communication platform. Integrating with Slack will enable users to share insights and collaborate with their teams.
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: No

## Next Steps
The next step is to establish partnerships with the selected SaaS and APIs, prioritizing those with affiliate/revenue-share opportunities. This will involve negotiating terms, setting up integrations, and promoting the partnerships to the Insight Loom user base.
